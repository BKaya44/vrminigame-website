Introduction

What is VRMinigame

This game is a Virtual Reality Game, you can interact with objects and play minigames like throwing the ball in a net or throwing a ring in a peg. The objective of this game is to have fun and not thing about having to complete a story. You can play the game in your own pace stop and come back anytime. The progress you make in the game will be saved on your PC. You will need a VR headset to play this game.

Low poly art style

This game has a low poly art style. Low Poly is a style of design that translates an image into a polygon tessellation using tree sided polygons. I used this art style because I felt like using something which was easy to create 3D models in. For the next time I would like to use more cartoonish art style.

3D Models

All 3d models are made by me using Blender. When looking back at the first models I created and the ones that are in game, I really improved creating 3D models. In the future I want to use Blender for animating the objects I made.

VR

The unique thing about this game is that you can interact with all objects using a VR headset. While owning a VR headset is a must to play the game, it really gives you the feeling that you are in the game. Creating a VR game was really a challenge since there are not many tutorials on how to create certain things for VR. Most things are done by testing it and tweaking the things that are not working correctly. For future game development I would rather create my own engine for a better learning experience.

Gameplay

Minigames

Basketball

Throw the ball into the net and get scores based on the combo you make. You can get a combo if you throw the ball into the net within 3 seconds of scoring. The higher the combo the higher the score you get each time you throw the ball in the net.

Handball

Here can throw a ball into a hole, depending on which hole you threw the ball in you get a score.

Ring throwing

Throw the ring towards the pegs and get scores depending on where you threw it into.

Sack throwing

Throw sacks against the cones and get a score based on how many you threw over.
